About Me

Hi there! I'm Saloni, a passionate and driven Information technology student with a knack for building innovative solutions. My journey into programming started in 9th grade, where I discovered the world of Java and fell in love with the art of problem-solving. This early spark has fueled my ambition to dive deeper into technology and create impactful projects.

I specialize in web development, data structures and algorithms, and enjoy exploring emerging technologies like AI and IoT. Through projects like an Amazon Clone and a Mine Safety Bot, I've honed my skills in building scalable, user-centric solutions that solve real-world challenges.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new tools, learning through hands-on experiences, or collaborating on exciting projects with like-minded individuals. My goal is to continue growing as a developer while contributing to meaningful innovations that make a difference.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and connect with me on Instagram or LinkedIn. Let's create something amazing together!
